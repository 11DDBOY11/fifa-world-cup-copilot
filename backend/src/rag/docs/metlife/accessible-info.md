# MetLife Stadium — Accessibility Information

## Accessible Restrooms
- All restrooms at MetLife Stadium are ADA accessible.
- Family and companion-care restrooms are located near Sections 104, 124, 143, and 208.

## Wheelchair Services
- Complimentary wheelchair escorts are available at all gates. Ask any staff member.
- Accessible seating is located on all levels of the stadium.
- Lot C is the primary ADA parking area. It is located 100 yards from Gate C.
