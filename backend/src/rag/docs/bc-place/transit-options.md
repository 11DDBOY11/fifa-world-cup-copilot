# BC Place — Transit Options / Transport Transit

## SkyTrain (Recommended)
- **Stadium-Chinatown Station**: Exposition Line. Exit station and walk 250 meters to Gate A.
- **Yaletown-Roundhouse Station**: Canada Line. Walk 600 meters to Gate D.

## Ferry / Water Shuttle
- False Creek Ferries stop at the Plaza of Nations dock, located 400 meters south of the stadium.
