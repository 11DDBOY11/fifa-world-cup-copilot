# BC Place — Accessibility Info / Infos d'Accessibilité

## Accessible Facilities
- All restrooms at BC Place are fully accessible.
- Universal gender-neutral restrooms with adult changing tables are available at Section 213 and Section 244.

## Mobility Services
- Wheelchair access is available via ramps at Gate C.
- Elevators are located at all gate plazas.
- Sensory-friendly kits and noise-cancelling headphones are available for loan at Guest Services.
