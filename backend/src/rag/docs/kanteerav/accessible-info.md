# Sree Kanteerava Stadium — Accessibility Information

## Accessible Restrooms
- Wheelchair-accessible restrooms are available near all four gates.
- Family and companion restrooms are located near Gate C and Gate A.

## Wheelchair Services
- Wheelchair loan available at Gate C (ground floor, staffed booth).
- Designated wheelchair seating zones in the lower stand, sections 5 and 6.

## Ramps and Lifts
- Ramped access at Gate A and Gate C — gradients under 8 degrees.
- Passenger lifts available inside the stadium connecting ground level to upper tiers.

## Accessible Parking
- 100 reserved accessible parking bays within 40 metres of Gate C.
- Drop-off/pick-up zone for vehicles with disability permits on the south access road.

## Assistance
- Trained accessibility stewards available at all gates.
- Audio assistance devices available on request at Gate A information desk.
