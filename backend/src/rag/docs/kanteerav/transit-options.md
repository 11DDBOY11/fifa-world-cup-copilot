# Sree Kanteerava Stadium — Transit Options

## Namma Metro (Recommended)
- **MG Road Station** (Purple Line): 800 metres from Gate A — approximately 10 minutes walking.
- **Cubbon Park Station** (Purple Line): 600 metres from Gate C — approximately 8 minutes walking.
- Metro runs every 5–10 minutes on match days. Last metro departs around 22:30.

## BMTC Bus
- Several BMTC routes (335E, 500C, KBS-5) stop on Kasturba Road, 200 metres from Gate A.
- Frequency increases on match days.

## Auto-Rickshaw / Cab
- Ola and Uber available throughout Bangalore. Estimated fare from Majestic Bus Stand: ₹80–120.
- Designated pick-up/drop-off zone on Palace Road (Gate B side).

## By Road
- Distance from Kempegowda International Airport: approximately 38 km (45–60 minutes depending on traffic).
- Parking: limited on-site parking; fans are encouraged to use public transit or park-and-ride at MG Road Metro.
