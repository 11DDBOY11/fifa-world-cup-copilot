# Sree Kanteerava Stadium — Gate Map

## Overview
Sree Kanteerava Stadium has four main entry gates: Gate A, Gate B, Gate C, and Gate D.

## Gate Locations
- **Gate A**: North side — main entrance facing Kasturba Road. Recommended for fans arriving from MG Road Metro Station (800 metres walk).
- **Gate B**: East side — near the officials' and media entrance. Close to the parking zone on Palace Road side.
- **Gate C**: South side — recommended for fans arriving from Cubbon Park Metro Station (600 metres walk). Also closest to the accessible parking bays (within 40 metres).
- **Gate D**: West side — near the VIP and hospitality boxes entrance.

## Entry Timing
Gates open 2.5 hours before kick-off. Fans are advised to arrive early to clear security screening.

## Nearby Landmarks
- MG Road Metro Station: 800 m from Gate A
- Cubbon Park Metro Station: 600 m from Gate C
- KSCA offices: opposite Gate B
